What is Rythmi?


Here at Rythmi our main goal is to unite the world with music. We believe that the first step is to help you learn how to play. A problem that many people have is that "They ain't got rhythm," however, that is a skill that can be learned. With the Rythmi metronome, you will be able to easily learn to stay on tempo, using its two main features:

Realtime Response

The Rythmi metronome will show you whether you are rushing, dragging, or on beat while you are playing!

Review Your Progress

See how far you have come on the review page!
